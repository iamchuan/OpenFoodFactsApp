Data Source:

Open Food Facts is a free, open, collbarative database of food products from around the world, with ingredients, allergens, nutrition facts and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.It is a non-profit association of volunteers.

By now, 5000+ contributors have added 100 000+ products from 150 countries using our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

Tools:

  • Built using Shiny by RStudio

  • Code available on GitHub
